About Gumasta License

The Gumasta License, also known as the Shop & Establishment License, is a mandatory registration required for any business operating from a commercial or home-based establishment. It is governed by the respective state's Shop and Establishment Act.

This license regulates working conditions, hours of operation, wages, holidays, and other employment conditions for people working in shops, commercial establishments, hotels, restaurants, theaters, and other public amusement centers. It is one of the most important local licenses for any business in India.

Documents Required for Gumasta License

Common documents needed for Shop & Establishment Registration

Identity & Business Proof

  • ✓ PAN Card of Owner/Business
  • ✓ Aadhaar Card of Proprietor/Partner
  • ✓ Passport Size Photographs (2 copies)
  • ✓ Business Name & Nature of Business

Address Proof of Establishment

  • ✓ Rent Agreement (if rented premises)
  • ✓ No Objection Certificate (NOC) from owner
  • ✓ Latest Electricity Bill / Water Bill
  • ✓ Property Tax Receipt (if owned)
  • ✓ Municipal Khata Certificate

Additional Documents

  • ✓ GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)
  • ✓ Partnership Deed (for partnership firms)
  • ✓ Certificate of Incorporation (for companies)
  • ✓ Employee Details (if any)
  • ✓ Previous License (for renewal)

Note: Document requirements may vary slightly by state and local municipality. We will guide you based on your specific location and business type. The license is typically valid for 1 to 5 years and requires renewal before expiry.
