What is Gazette Notification?

Gazette Notification is an official publication by the Government of India used to announce legal information such as name change, address change, or other public notices. It acts as a valid legal proof of your updated identity.

Publishing your name change in the Gazette is mandatory for updating your name in official documents like Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport, and other government records. It ensures legal recognition and avoids future complications.
