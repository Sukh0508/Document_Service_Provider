What is Street Vendor License?

A Street Vendor Licence (also called a Hawker Licence) is an official permission given by the local municipal authority to sell goods on streets, footpaths, or public places legally.

It protects vendors from legal issues, prevents unnecessary fines, and allows them to run their business smoothly and safely in designated areas approved by the government.
