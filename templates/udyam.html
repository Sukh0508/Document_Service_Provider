About Udyam Registration

Udyam Registration is the new process for MSME registration introduced by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Government of India. It replaced the earlier Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM) system from July 1, 2020.

Any business entity classified as Micro, Small or Medium Enterprise based on investment in plant & machinery or equipment and annual turnover can register under Udyam. The registration is completely online, free of cost, and provides a unique Udyam Registration Number (URN).

Documents Required for Udyam Registration

Simple documentation - mostly based on self-declaration

Identity & Business Proof

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (of Proprietor/Partner/Director)
  • ✓ PAN Card (of Business/Individual)
  • ✓ Business Name & Address
  • ✓ GSTIN (if applicable)

Investment & Turnover Details

  • ✓ Investment in Plant & Machinery/Equipment
  • ✓ Annual Turnover of Business
  • ✓ Bank Account Details (optional)
  • ✓ Previous UAM Number (if any)

For Partnership/Company

  • ✓ Partnership Deed / COI
  • ✓ Aadhaar & PAN of all Partners/Directors
  • ✓ Authorization Letter

Note: Udyam Registration is completely free of cost. No fees are charged by the government. Registration is based on self-declaration, and no documents need to be uploaded to the Udyam portal (only Aadhaar number is required). However, we recommend keeping supporting documents ready.
