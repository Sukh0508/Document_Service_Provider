About Domicile Certificate

A Domicile Certificate is an official document that proves a person is a permanent resident of a particular state or union territory in India. It is issued by the state government or local authorities.

This certificate is important for applying to government jobs, educational institutions, and various state government schemes. It also helps in availing reservation benefits and proving residential status for legal and administrative purposes.
